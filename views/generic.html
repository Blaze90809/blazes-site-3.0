A little about me

In my current job at Turnkey Corrections, I work as a full-stack developer focused on software in the corrections industry.

Our organization's newest front-end software is developed with the latest versions of Angular. Our back-end code is written in GoLang, and our database management system is MySQL. We stay true with Angular in mobile development, as well, using the Ionic framework for IOS and Android development.

I have also played around with other front-end frameworks. As an avid cross-country ski racer, the side project I use the most is my Nordic Race Track application. The application was developed in React, with a back-end of Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.

Working as a full-stack developer in my day job can be a challenge; you do not have much time to specialize in one area of software. However, it is rewarding to know you are working with every part of a program. If you have a side-project needing help from an experienced developer like myself, do not hesitate to reach out.
