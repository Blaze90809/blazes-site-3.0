My Experience in Software

Hello, I am Blaze Fugina, a software developer based in the Twin Cities.

After working in the field of journalism for most of my 20s, I decided to make a career switch to software development. After graduating from Prime Digital Academy, a programming boot camp based in the Twin Cities, I was hired to my current position as a front-end developer at Turnkey Corrections/Three Square Market.

Aside from my fulltime work, I also enjoy cross-country skiing, snowboarding and playing tennis. As you can tell, I love the cold and snowy winters we often get in the Upper Midwest.

I am always looking for a new challenge, so feel free to reach out with any requests for freelance help. Or just to say hi. My contact information is available on the contacts page.
